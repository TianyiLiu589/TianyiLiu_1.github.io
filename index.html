Airsoft

Airsoft is a team-based competitive sport that simulates military tactics and combat. Players use air-powered guns, which closely resemble real firearms, to shoot small plastic pellets commonly referred to as BBs. The sport emphasizes tactical strategy, teamwork, and individual skill, allowing participants to experience the thrill of combat-like scenarios in a safe environment.Airsoft games can be played in various settings, including outdoor woods, urban environment simulations, and indoor facilities.
